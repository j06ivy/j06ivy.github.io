About Pro Gamers Group

At Pro Gamers Group (PGG), we connect gaming brands with players worldwide. As a leading force in gaming hardware retail and distribution, we provide brands with direct access to the gaming community, ensuring products reach the right audience through our extensive network of online stores, marketplaces, and retail partners. We don’t just sell gaming products—we help shape the industry. Through our Retail Media Solutions, we offer strategic marketing opportunities backed by real-time sales data, allowing brands to grow their presence and engage with gamers effectively.
